# Preflight — Demo Video Script

**Target length:** 2–3 minutes  
**Format:** screen recording with voiceover

---

## [0:00 – 0:20] The problem

> "AI tools have made it faster than ever to build things. Cursor, Lovable, Bolt — you can go from idea to working app in a day."

> "The problem is: it's also faster than ever to build the wrong thing."

> "You still have to decide what to build and what to validate first. Most people skip that step."

> "Preflight is the five-minute checkpoint that happens before you open your editor."

---

## [0:20 – 0:35] Show the landing page

*[Navigate to the landing page]*

> "This is Preflight. The pre-shipping checkpoint for AI builders."

> "Paste your idea. Answer four questions. Get your riskiest assumption and a 24–48 hour validation experiment."

> "Let me show you what that looks like."

*[Click 'Start a Preflight Check']*

---

## [0:35 – 1:00] Paste an idea

*[Navigate to /new]*

> "I'll dogfood it — I'll run Preflight on Preflight itself: a pre-shipping checkpoint that helps AI builders spot risky assumptions before they build the wrong thing."

*[Paste the idea into the form]*

> "Just plain language. No frameworks, no templates."

*[Click Continue]*

---

## [1:00 – 1:30] Answer 4 questions

*[Navigate to /clarify]*

> "Preflight asks four focused questions:"

*[Briefly read each question as you fill them in]*

> "Who uses this first? What do they do today instead? What moment hurts most? What would make it worth trying this week?"

> "These four answers are what make the output sharp. The more specific, the better."

*[Click 'Generate Preflight Check']*

> "Takes about ten seconds."

---

## [1:30 – 1:55] Show the Preflight Score

*[Navigate to /check/[id]]*

> "Here's the Preflight Check."

*[Point to the score]*

> "Preflight Score: 71. Recommendation: Validate first."

> "Important — this isn't a verdict on whether the idea is good. The score reflects how clearly the idea is framed and how ready it is to validate. It breaks down across five dimensions — user clarity, problem sharpness, evidence strength, validation readiness, and build focus."

> "This tells me: the user and problem are sharp, but I have almost no evidence yet — so validate before building."

---

## [1:55 – 2:15] Show Riskiest Assumption

*[Point to the Riskiest Assumption section]*

> "This is the most important output."

> "Solo AI builders will actually stop to run a structured check — instead of opening Cursor and building immediately."

> "That's the single belief most likely to make the whole thing fail. And it's something I can test this week, without writing any code."

---

## [2:15 – 2:35] Show Validation Experiment

*[Point to the Validation Experiment section]*

> "And here's how."

> "Catch 8 builders the moment they have a new idea, send them Preflight, and count how many run a check before writing any code."

> "Concrete steps, a measurable success signal, time required — 24 to 48 hours."

*[Click 'Copy experiment']*

> "One click to copy the whole thing. Drop it in Notion, Slack, wherever your workflow lives."

---

## [2:35 – 2:50] Share the public artifact

*[Click Share or navigate to /share/[id]]*

> "When you're ready, you can publish a clean read-only artifact — share it with a co-founder, an advisor, or just to have a record of where your thinking was before you built."

*[Show the share page]*

> "No editing controls. Just the output."

---

## [2:50 – 3:00] Close

> "Preflight doesn't tell you your idea is good or bad."

> "It shows you what still needs evidence before you build."

> "Before you build it — see what you're assuming."

*[End on landing page or share page]*

---

## Recording notes

- Use the demo-check (`/check/demo-check`) if you want a guaranteed clean result without live AI
- The public share artifact is at `/share/demo-check`
- Record at 1440×900 or 1280×800 for clarity
- Keep the voiceover conversational, not salesy
- No music needed — the product speaks for itself
